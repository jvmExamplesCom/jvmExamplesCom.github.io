Advantages of StatelessSessionBean

The main advantage of using a StatelessSessionBean is its ability to scale. Because the bean is stateless, it can service multiple clients concurrently without any additional overhead. Additionally, stateless beans are usually faster to process requests, as there is no need to maintain any state between requests. Finally, stateless beans can be clustered, allowing for increased scalability and performance. In addition, stateless beans are typically easier to develop and maintain than other types of EJBs, as there is less complexity involved with maintaining state.

Disadvantages of StatelessSessionBean

The primary disadvantage of using stateless beans is that they cannot provide any kind of stateful interaction with the client. This makes them less suited to applications that require more complex interactions, such as user registration and login. Additionally, while stateless beans can be clustered, they must be configured correctly and maintained in order to ensure high availability.
