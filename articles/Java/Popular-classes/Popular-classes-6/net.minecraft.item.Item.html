ItemTool is used for representing tools, such as swords, pickaxes, and hoes. In addition to the two main subclasses, the Item class also contains several other subclasses for representing specific items. For example, the ItemFood subclass is used for representing food items, such as bread and pork chops. The ItemArmor subclass is used for representing armor, such as leather and iron armor.
