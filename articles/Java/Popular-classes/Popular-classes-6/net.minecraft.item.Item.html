The Item class is responsible for providing the basic item definition in the game. It defines the item's name, type, physical attributes, and behavior. ItemTool is used for representing tools, such as swords, pickaxes, and hoes. For example, the ItemFood subclass is used for representing food items, such as bread and pork chops. The ItemArmor subclass is used for representing armor, such as leather and iron armor.
