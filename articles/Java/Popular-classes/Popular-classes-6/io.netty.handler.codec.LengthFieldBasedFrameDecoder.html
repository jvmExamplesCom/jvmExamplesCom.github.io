What is io.netty.handler.codec.LengthFieldBasedFrameDecoder?

The LengthFieldBasedFrameDecoder uses length fields in the header of the data being decoded to determine the boundaries for each frame. How Does the LengthFieldBasedFrameDecoder Work?

The LengthFieldBasedFrameDecoder works by parsing the bytes in a stream of data and looking for the length field in the header. This length field can be encoded in one of several ways, such as a fixed length field or a variable-length field that is indicated by a marker in the header. Once the length field is found, the decoder will begin reading the bytes until it reaches the specified length.
