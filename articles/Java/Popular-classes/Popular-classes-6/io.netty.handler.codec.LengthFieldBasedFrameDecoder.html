What is io.netty.handler.codec.LengthFieldBasedFrameDecoder?

How Does the LengthFieldBasedFrameDecoder Work?

The LengthFieldBasedFrameDecoder works by parsing the bytes in a stream of data and looking for the length field in the header. Once the length field is found, the decoder will begin reading the bytes until it reaches the specified length.
